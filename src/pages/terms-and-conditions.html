Frequently Asked Questions

Questions Answers
How do I commence permanent parking? You must sign this Agreement where indicated.
Your signed Agreement and payment must be submitted to Get Parked
How soon can I start permanent parking? You will be generally be able to start as soon as your Agreement is received and payment schedule setup. Our team will be in touch with you as soon as we have received your Agreement and payment. If you need to start ASAP, we recommend you email your Agreement and payment advice to us, and follow up with a call.
How do I collect my parking pass? Instructions will be supplied via email for how to gain access and begin parking.
Is there any minimum period that I must park for? It is necessary to park for a minimum period of one month. In addition to the minimum period, you must give one month’s written notice of your intention to cancel your account. Notice can be given in writing at any time.
How will I be invoiced? You will be invoiced one calendar month in advance. It is necessary to pay for your parking at the commencement of each month. If you have started part-way through a month, your first month’s account will be reconciled with a pro-rata charge. This means your first account may be larger than expected as your initial charges will be for one part-month and one full month in advance.
Can I add additional bays to my account? Yes, please email enquiries@getparked.com.au and discuss the additional requirement, the name of the new Customer along with their contact details, vehicle details and the date you would like them to commence.
How do I cancel my account? To cancel your account you will need to give one month’s written notice by email to enquiries@getparked.com.au
